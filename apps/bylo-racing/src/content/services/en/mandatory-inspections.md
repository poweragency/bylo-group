## What's checked in the inspection

The mandatory inspection covers:

- **Brake system** — efficiency, balance, disc condition
- **Steering and suspensions** — play, wear, alignment
- **Lighting and signaling** — headlights, indicators, stop, reverse
- **Tires** — wear, pressure, tread depth
- **Emissions** — exhaust gas analysis against regulatory thresholds
- **Body and frame** — condition
- **Internal safety** — seatbelts, airbags, anchor points

## Who it's for

- Private clients with cars due for inspection
- Companies with light commercial vehicles (vans, workshop vehicles, delivery vehicles) up to 3.5 tons
- Those wanting to combine inspection and scheduled service in a single visit to reduce downtime
