## Our body shop process

1. **Damage diagnosis** — full vehicle analysis, photos, transparent quote with breakdown of work and timing.
2. **Preparation and repair** — body work, parts replacement, frame straightening, filling and sanding.
3. **Color matching** — precise tone matching with professional spectrophotometer, to reproduce the paint identical to the original.
4. **Oven painting** — dedicated booth, water-based paints, controlled drying.
5. **Final check and delivery** — finish verification and delivery of the cleaned vehicle.

## What we don't do

- Vinyl wrapping is not a standard service.
- We don't restore heavily damaged frames on classic vehicles (refer to specialist body shops).
