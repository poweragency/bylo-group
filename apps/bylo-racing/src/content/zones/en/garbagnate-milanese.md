## From Garbagnate to our location

The two main routes are SP233 Varesina (through Lainate) and A9 Lainate-Como with the Lainate exit. Six kilometres from central Garbagnate, 11 minutes outside peak hours. For those leaving from the hospital area, the distance drops slightly: you join SS233 directly southbound toward Lainate-Mazzo. From Garbagnate-Bariana, the south-eastern village, SP527 is the better choice, running directly to Mazzo through Bollate.

## Garbagnate and its two worlds

Garbagnate has a dual profile: on one side the hospital hub, which brings thousands of healthcare workers, associated services and private medical practices into the municipality. On the other, an engineering fabric dating back to the 1960s and 70s, with small and medium-sized firms still active in light metallurgy, plastic moulding and precision machining. For Tommy By this means two distinct customer types: healthcare workers with irregular hours requiring flexibility on drop-off windows, and small businesses with light commercial fleets that need scheduled management.
