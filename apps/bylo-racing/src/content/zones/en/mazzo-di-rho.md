## A workshop in your own neighborhood

For Mazzo residents the differentiating factor isn't travel time (zero) but the nature of the relationship with the workshop: we know each other, you walk in without ceremony, paperwork (inspections, post-collision claims) gets handled face-to-face without online appointment systems. That's the upside of a workshop that lives in the same neighborhood as its customers.

## What it means to have workshop and body shop on the same street

In many cases a car needs both mechanical maintenance and a bit of body shop work (a deep scratch, a paint touch-up, a bumper repair). Without an integrated facility that means two separate appointments, two transport trips, two job timelines. With us, in many cases, the work is done in parallel within the same building and the car is returned ready in a single time window.

## Typical services for neighborhood residents

- **Quick walk-in checks** (lights, tire pressure, dashboard warning lights, fluid levels)
- **MOT-equivalent inspections** same-day, usually planned around each vehicle's biennial deadline
- **Seasonal tire service** with on-site storage — convenient for those without garage space
- **Coordinated maintenance across multiple household vehicles** (work car + weekend car + scooter)
