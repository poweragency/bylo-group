## Vanzago to Mazzo di Rho route

Five kilometres on SP109 south-eastbound, 9 minutes outside peak hours (12-15 minutes between 7:30 and 9 AM). From Mantegazza the timing is slightly longer due to the internal stretch. As an alternative, those coming from southern Vanzago can route through Pregnana and avoid the central SP109 stretch when it's congested.

## Vanzago, the Oasis and the neighborhood

Vanzago is one of the most residential and least-trafficked municipalities in Tommy By's catchment. The WWF Oasis at the Bosco di Vanzago (one of the very few forest reserves in northern Milan) shapes the territory in urban planning terms too: detached houses, gardens, a slower pace of life than the towns along the Sempione axis. For Tommy By the typical Vanzago customer is the resident with one or two family cars to maintain over the medium-to-long term, with a preference for a trusted workshop they don't switch often.
