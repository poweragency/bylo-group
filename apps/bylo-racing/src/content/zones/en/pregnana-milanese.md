## From Pregnana to our location

The standard route is SP109 eastbound toward Mazzo di Rho. Three kilometres, 6 minutes. For those leaving from the FS station area the timing is similar. From southern Pregnana (Via Roma area) you can follow SP172 through the farmland toward Cornaredo, slightly longer but smoother during the morning rush.

## Pregnana between residential and small-scale production

Pregnana is a small residential municipality with a production area on the Vanzago border, hosting small-scale manufacturing and logistics companies. The presence of the train station on the Milan-Novara line makes Pregnana a structural commuter point toward Milan, and this translates into a share of the local clientele working in the city five days a week. For Tommy By the specific advantage in serving Pregnana is the combination of "physical proximity" (3 km, easily reachable with a short ride) and "train available" (for those who want to continue the day without a replacement car).

## Services most requested by Pregnana customers

- **Scheduled services and inspections** on family cars
