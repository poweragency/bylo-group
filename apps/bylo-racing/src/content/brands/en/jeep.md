## Jeep models we handle

- **Avenger** (electric and petrol) — new-generation city car/B-SUV, Stellantis CMP platform
- **Renegade** — B-SUV, shares base with Fiat 500X
- **Compass** — C-SUV, also 4xe hybrid and full electric
- **Wrangler** — historic 4x4, also 4xe plug-in
- **Grand Cherokee** — D-E SUV, also 4xe

## Specifics

- **4x4 system** — Active Drive, Active Drive Low, Selec-Terrain — diagnostics with FCA/Stellantis compatible tools
- **4xe system** plug-in hybrid — 11-17 kWh battery pack, electric motor on the rear axle
- **Trail Rated** — models homologated for off-road use with tighter specs on differentials and dampers
- **Uconnect** — Stellantis infotainment shared with Fiat and Alfa Romeo
