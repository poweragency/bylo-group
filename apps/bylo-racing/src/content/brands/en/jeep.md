## Jeep models we handle

- **Avenger** (electric and petrol) — new-generation city-car / B-SUV on the Stellantis CMP platform
- **Renegade** — B-SUV, shares its base with the Fiat 500X
- **Compass** — C-SUV, including the 4xe hybrid and the full-electric version
- **Wrangler** — the historic 4x4, including the 4xe plug-in
- **Grand Cherokee** — D-E SUV, including the 4xe
- **Gladiator** — Trail Rated pick-up

## Technical specifics

- **GME T4 2.0** turbo petrol engines (Wrangler, Grand Cherokee)
- **MultiJet 1.6 / 2.0 / 2.2** diesel (phasing out)
- **4x4 system** — Active Drive, Active Drive Low, Selec-Terrain — diagnosed with FCA / Stellantis-compatible tools
- **4xe system** plug-in hybrid — 11-17 kWh battery pack, electric motor on the rear axle
- **Trail Rated** — models homologated for off-road use, with stricter specifications on differentials, dampers and approach angles
- **Uconnect** — Stellantis infotainment shared with Fiat and Alfa Romeo
