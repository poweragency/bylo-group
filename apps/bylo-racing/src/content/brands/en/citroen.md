faq:
  - q: Do Progressive Hydraulic Cushions require special maintenance?
    a: >-
      PHCs are dampers with a dual hydraulic chamber that don't require maintenance different from standard. Replacements with original Citroën parts (or certified equivalents) preserve the brand's signature "soft" behavior. Diagnosis of a worn PHC is done with the usual damper test bench.

## Citroën models we handle

- **C3** and **C3 Aircross** — city car and mini-SUV
- **C4** and **ë-C4** electric — C-segment
- **C5 Aircross** — SUV including PHEV
- **Berlingo / Jumpy / Jumper** — LCVs (including ë- electric)
- **C5 X** — flagship with Progressive Hydraulic Cushions technology

## Technical specifics

- **PureTech 1.2** shared with Peugeot — see notes on the timing belt
- **BlueHDi** common-rail with SCR (AdBlue)
- **ë-C4 electric** — CMP platform shared with Peugeot e-208/e-2008, Opel Mokka-e
- **PHC suspension** — dampers with dual hydraulic chamber (see FAQ)
- **Infotainment** — 10" touchscreen with wireless Apple CarPlay / Android Auto
