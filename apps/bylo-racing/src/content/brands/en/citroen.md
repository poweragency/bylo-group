faq:
  - q: Do you have experience with the oil-bath timing belt of the PureTech 1.2?
    a: >-
      Yes. On pre-2019 PureTech 1.2 engines, the oil-bath timing belt was prone to early degradation, releasing debris that clogs the oil pump. Stellantis revised the belt compound and, in some cases, issued a recall campaign. We carry out a preventive inspection during the scheduled service and, on at-risk cars, recommend the upgrade to the new-generation belt with a shorter interval.
  - q: Do the Progressive Hydraulic Cushions need special maintenance?
    a: >-
      The PHC dampers use a dual hydraulic chamber and do not call for any maintenance beyond what a standard damper requires. Replacement with original Citroën parts (or certified equivalents) preserves the brand's hallmark "soft" ride. A worn PHC is diagnosed on the usual damper test rig.
  - q: Do you also service older Citroën models (C2, C3 Pluriel, Picasso)?
    a: >-
      Yes. Older Citroëns still come through the workshop regularly. The challenge is parts availability for some specific components, but for the C3, C4 and Picasso of the last fifteen years, parts are still well distributed. For rarer cars (C6, the pre-spin-off DS3) we recommend confirming availability before scheduling the work.

## Citroën models we handle

- **C3** and **C3 Aircross** — city car and mini-SUV
- **C4** and **ë-C4** electric — C-segment
- **C5 Aircross** — SUV including the PHEV
- **Berlingo / Jumpy / Jumper** — LCVs (including the ë- electric versions)
- **C5 X** — the flagship with Progressive Hydraulic Cushions

## Technical specifics

- **PureTech 1.2** turbo shared with Peugeot and Opel — see the note on the oil-bath belt
- **BlueHDi** common-rail diesel with SCR (AdBlue)
- **CMP platform** — the base for the ë-C4, shared with Peugeot e-208 / e-2008 and Opel Mokka-e
- **EMP2 platform** — the base for the C5 Aircross and C5 X
- **PHC suspension** — dual-chamber hydraulic dampers (see FAQ)
- **Advanced Comfort** — Citroën package focused on seating, acoustic insulation and suspension
- **Infotainment** — 10" touchscreen with wireless Apple CarPlay and Android Auto
