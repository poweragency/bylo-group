## Opel models we see most often

- **Corsa** (both GM-base and post-2019 Stellantis), including the Corsa-e electric
- **Astra** (J, K and L) and **Astra Sports Tourer**
- **Mokka / Mokka X / Mokka-e** — compact SUV
- **Grandland (X)** — C-SUV, also PHEV
- **Crossland (X)** — B-SUV
- **Insignia** — D-segment (out of range from 2022)
- **Zafira** and **Combo** — MPV and LCV

## Typical technical points

- **GM generation (pre-2018)** — 1.0/1.4/1.6 turbo petrol, 1.6 CDTI diesel, GM parts
- **Stellantis generation (post-2018)** — PureTech, BlueHDi engines, parts shared with Peugeot/Citroën
- **OnStar system** discontinued on Opels in late 2020 — no longer updated
- **Mokka-e and Corsa-e** electric — CMP platform shared with Peugeot e-208/e-2008
