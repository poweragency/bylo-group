## FIAT technical areas we cover

- **Historic FIRE engines** — 1.2 and 1.4 8V/16V, widely found on pre-2017 Panda and Punto
- **MultiAir** — electro-hydraulic valve actuation on the 1.4 16V (500X, Tipo) and the 1.75 (Stelvio-derived)
- **MultiJet diesel** — 1.3 (phased out) and 1.6/2.0 still in use
- **TwinAir 0.9** — FIAT-specific turbocharged twin (500, Panda, 500L)
- **T-Jet** — older-generation 1.4 turbo petrol
- **Stop & Start** — correct EFB / AGM battery management, requiring coding after replacement
- **UConnect 5"/7"** — Stellantis infotainment with Apple CarPlay and Android Auto

## The FIAT jobs customers ask for most

- Panda and 500 scheduled service with Selenia oil to FCA specification
- Timing belt replacement on TwinAir and MultiJet engines
- Engine ECU and Body Computer (BCM) diagnostics
- Battery replacement with BCM coding for Stop & Start
- Service and brakes on Doblò and Ducato vans for local tradespeople
- Same-day MOT-equivalent inspection, up to 3.5 tonnes
