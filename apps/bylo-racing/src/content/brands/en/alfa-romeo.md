faq:
  - q: Do the 1.4 MultiAir engines on the Giulietta suffer from oil consumption issues?
    a: >-
      On first-generation MultiAir units (Giulietta roughly 2010-2014) we have seen above-average oil consumption on some cars, together with a handful of cases of early timing chain wear. We run electronic diagnostics during the scheduled service and flag any follow-up work (chain or valve replacement). On the second-generation MultiAir the issue is marginal.
  - q: Do you manage the DNA / DNA Pro selector and the Q4 driveline on the Stelvio and Tonale?
    a: >-
      Yes. The DNA system controls engine mapping, steering and suspension, and on Q4 versions it also manages torque distribution between the front and rear axles through an electronically controlled multi-plate clutch. We diagnose faults with Stellantis-compatible equipment and replace the Q4 transfer-case oil at the prescribed interval.
  - q: Does the Giulia Quadrifoglio need special maintenance for the Ferrari-derived engine?
    a: >-
      The 2.9 V6 biturbo on the Quadrifoglio was co-engineered with Ferrari technicians at the Balocco technical centre and runs on tighter intervals: engine oil every 15,000 km rather than 30,000, turbocharger belt checks and close attention to oil temperature. We can carry out the standard scheduled services and refer more complex work to a specialist centre.

## Alfa Romeo models we handle

- **Giulietta** — historic best-seller (production ended in 2020 but still widely on the road)
- **MiTo** — city car (out of production since 2018, still frequent)
- **Giulia** — sporty D-segment (including the Quadrifoglio)
- **Stelvio** — D-segment SUV (including the Quadrifoglio)
- **Tonale** — C-segment SUV (including the PHEV Q4)
- **Junior** (formerly Milano) — new-generation B-SUV, also available as a full EV

## Key technologies

- **MultiAir** — electro-hydraulic valve actuation (1.4 and 1.75 engines)
- **GME T4 2.0 turbo** (Giulia / Stelvio) — 200 or 280 hp, Q4 AWD on the top trims
- **2.2 MultiJet diesel** — common-rail unit with DPF management and AdBlue on Euro 6d cars
- **2.9 V6 biturbo** Ferrari-derived (Quadrifoglio) — 510 hp, dedicated maintenance schedule
- **DNA / DNA Pro** — drive-mode selector for engine, suspension and steering
- **Connect 8.8"** — infotainment with Apple CarPlay and Android Auto
- **Q4 PHEV system** on the Tonale — shares its base with the Jeep Compass 4xe and Renegade 4xe
