## Alfa Romeo models we handle

- **Giulietta** — historic best-seller (production ended 2020 but still widely on the road)
- **MiTo** — city car (out 2018, still frequent)
- **Giulia** — sporty D-segment (also Quadrifoglio)
- **Stelvio** — D-SUV (also Quadrifoglio)
- **Tonale** — C-SUV (also Q4 PHEV)
- **Junior** (formerly Milano) — new-gen B-SUV, also electric

## Key technologies

- **MultiAir** — electro-hydraulic valve actuation (1.4 and 1.75 engines)
- **2.0 turbo petrol** (Giulia/Stelvio) — 200/280 HP, Q4 AWD on top versions
- **2.9 V6 biturbo** Ferrari-derived (Quadrifoglio) — 510 HP, specific maintenance
- **DNA / DNA Pro** — drive mode selector for engine, suspension and steering
- **JTDM** common-rail diesel (phasing out)
- **Q4 PHEV system** on Tonale — shared base with Jeep Compass 4xe and Renegade 4xe
