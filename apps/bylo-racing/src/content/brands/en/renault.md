faq:
  - q: Do you handle the E-Tech full hybrid and plug-in system with the multimode gearbox?
    a: >-
      Yes. E-Tech is a parallel hybrid set-up with two electric motors and a clutchless multimode gearbox (engagements controlled electronically). Conceptually it is close to Toyota's HSD, but the gearbox is Renault-specific and the diagnostic logic differs. We service E-Tech models to Renault's manual intervals and verify the state of health of the HV battery as part of the check.
  - q: Do you handle the EDC dual-clutch gearbox?
    a: >-
      Yes. The EDC (Efficient Dual Clutch) is the Getrag 6DCT fitted to the Clio, Captur and Mégane. We renew the gearbox oil at the prescribed intervals to prevent cold-start slipping and diagnose adaptations after any related work. On first-generation EDC gearboxes (Clio IV, Mégane III) we check the clutch sensors, which tend to be the first failure point.
  - q: Do you also work on Dacia, Renault Trafic and Master vans?
    a: >-
      Yes. Dacia shares most of its mechanicals with Renault and has its own dedicated page. For the Trafic and Master we handle scheduled services, brakes, tyres and diagnostics. On special-bodied vans (refrigeration units, flatbed conversions) we cover the automotive side and leave the conversion-specific work to the original equipment supplier.

## Renault models we handle

- **City cars and B-segment** — Twingo, Clio, Captur (including the E-Tech hybrids)
- **C and D segment** — Mégane (including the E-Tech and the electric version), Arkana, Austral
- **Commercial vehicles** — Kangoo, Trafic, Master (Phase 2 and Phase 3)
- **Older models** — Clio II and III, Mégane II and III still on the road

## Typical technical points

- **TCe engines** — turbo petrol 90 / 100 / 130 Energy (timing belt with a specific interval)
- **dCi / Blue dCi 1.5** — common-rail diesel, robust but with attention to injectors and turbocharger beyond 200,000 km
- **EDC gearbox** — 6DCT dual-clutch (see FAQ)
- **E-Tech system** — full hybrid or plug-in with the dedicated multimode gearbox (Clio, Captur, Mégane)
- **CLIP diagnostics** — Renault-compatible multi-brand diagnostic tooling
- **EASY LINK / R-Link** — infotainment with OBD updates for maps and profiles
