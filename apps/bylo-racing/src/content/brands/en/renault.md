## Renault models we handle

- **City and B-segment** — Twingo, Clio, Captur (including E-Tech hybrids)
- **C and D segment** — Mégane (including E-Tech and electric), Arkana, Austral
- **LCVs** — Kangoo, Trafic, Master (Phase 2 and Phase 3)
- **Older models** — Clio II/III, Mégane II/III still on the road

## Typical technical points

- **TCe timing belt** — turbo petrol TCe 90, 100, 130 engines have a specific belt interval
- **1.5 dCi** — historic Renault diesel, robust but with attention on injectors and turbo after 200,000 km
- **E-Tech system** — full hybrid or plug-in hybrid with dedicated multimode gearbox
- **R-Link / EasyConnect** — infotainment with OBD-based updates for map and profile management
