## Škoda models we handle

- **Citigo / Fabia** — city car and B-segment
- **Scala / Kamiq** — B-C segment, recent design
- **Octavia / Octavia Combi** — historic best-seller, C/D segment
- **Karoq / Kodiaq** — C/D SUV (Kodiaq also 7 seats)
- **Superb** — E-segment, both sedan and wagon
- **Enyaq** — electric SUV, MEB platform

## Common VW Group technologies

- **TSI engines** — turbo petrol direct injection (1.0, 1.5, 2.0)
- **TDI engines** — turbo diesel common-rail (1.6, 2.0)
- **DSG** — dual-clutch 6 and 7-speed gearbox
- **MQB** — modular platform shared with VW, Audi, SEAT
- **MEB** — electric platform for Enyaq (shared with ID.4)
- **MIB3** — infotainment with OTA updates
