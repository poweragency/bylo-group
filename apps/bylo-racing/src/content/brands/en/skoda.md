faq:
  - q: Do the 1.5 TSI evo engines have known oil-consumption or "kangaroo jump" issues?
    a: >-
      Yes. On first-generation 1.5 TSI evo engines (2017-2019) some units showed high oil consumption and the cold-start "kangaroo jumps". VW Group released firmware updates and, in some cases, piston ring replacements. We run oil-consumption diagnostics during the scheduled service and flag any corrective work if the car is still under warranty.
  - q: Can I service the electric Škoda Enyaq on the MEB platform here?
    a: >-
      Yes. The Enyaq is built on the VW Group's MEB platform, shared with the ID.4, ID.5 and Audi Q4 e-tron. We carry out standard maintenance (cabin filter, brakes, tyres, climate control, HV battery coolant) and run HV system diagnostics with VAS / ODIS-compatible tooling. For complex cell-level battery work we refer the car to an authorised centre.
  - q: Do you handle the DSG gearbox and DSG oil changes across the VW group?
    a: >-
      Yes. The six-speed wet-clutch DSG (DQ250), the seven-speed dry-clutch DSG (DQ200) and the seven-speed wet-clutch DSG (DQ500) all need oil and filter renewal at specific intervals (60,000 km for the wet-clutch units). We carry out the full procedure with the dedicated VW fluid and reset the adaptations afterwards.

## Škoda models we handle

- **Citigo / Fabia** — city car and B-segment
- **Scala / Kamiq** — B/C segment with recent styling
- **Octavia / Octavia Combi** — historic best-seller, C/D segment
- **Karoq / Kodiaq** — C/D SUVs (the Kodiaq also as a seven-seater)
- **Superb** — E-segment, both saloon and estate
- **Enyaq** — electric SUV on the MEB platform

## Common VW Group technologies

- **TSI engines** — turbo petrol with direct injection (1.0, 1.5, 2.0)
- **TDI engines** — turbo diesel common-rail (1.6, 2.0) with SCR / AdBlue
- **DSG** — dual-clutch six- and seven-speed gearbox (see FAQ)
- **MQB** — modular platform shared with VW, Audi and SEAT
- **MEB** — electric platform for the Enyaq (shared with the ID.4)
- **MIB3** — infotainment with OTA updates
- **Simply Clever** — Škoda's hallmark practical solutions and very competitive parts pricing
