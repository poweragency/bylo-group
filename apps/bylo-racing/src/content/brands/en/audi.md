  - q: Do you have VAS/ODIS diagnostic equipment for Audi ECUs?
    a: >-
      We use diagnostic tooling compatible with the VAG group's VAS5054/ODIS protocols (Audi, VW, Skoda, Seat). This lets us read and manage the engine, the S-tronic DSG transmission, ABS/ESP, climate control and the ADAS systems. More involved server-side SVM coding can be referred to the authorised dealer when needed.
  - q: Does the wet-clutch S-tronic gearbox need any specific maintenance?
    a: >-
      Yes. The wet-clutch 7-speed DSG (DQ500 on the more powerful Audi quattro models) needs an oil and filter change roughly every 60,000 km, separate from the engine service. The 6-speed wet-clutch DSG (DQ250) follows the same interval. We carry out the full procedure with the dedicated fluid and reset the gearbox adaptations afterwards.
  - q: My post-2018 A3 TFSI has a gasoline particulate filter (GPF) — does it need attention?
    a: >-
      Yes. From Euro 6d-Temp onwards every TFSI Audi carries a GPF (gasoline particulate filter), and on heavily urban duty cycles we sometimes see frequent regeneration events. We check the GPF status via diagnostics and, if needed, run a forced regeneration cycle in the workshop.

## Audi technical areas we cover

- **TFSI petrol engines** — 1.0, 1.5 and 2.0 with direct injection, with GPF from 2018 onwards
- **TDI diesel engines** — 2.0 and 3.0 V6 common-rail, SCR with AdBlue
- **S-tronic DSG gearbox** — 6-speed wet-clutch DQ250, 7-speed wet-clutch DQ500 on the higher-output quattro models
- **Quattro Ultra** — on-demand all-wheel drive that disengages below set torque thresholds (A4, A5, Q5)
- **MQB and MLB Evo platforms** — shared across the VW Group, with broad parts availability
- **Service Inspection** — every 30,000 km or 24 months, with intermediate LongLife oil changes governed by the oil-quality sensor
