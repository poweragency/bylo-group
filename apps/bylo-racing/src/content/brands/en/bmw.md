    a: >-
      Yes. We read CBS through iDrive, identify which items are due (engine oil, micro-filter, front and rear pads, parking brake, brake fluid) and after the job we run the correct reset with compatible diagnostic equipment. The cluster-only manual reset works on a subset of items and does not respect the BMW logic for the next interval.
  - q: Are there known weak points on the B-series engines (B48, B58)?
    a: >-
      The four-cylinder B48 and six-cylinder B58 are robust units, but at high mileage (beyond 150,000 km) some cars show above-average oil consumption and, on early production runs, occasional timing-chain wear. We monitor oil consumption during the scheduled service and inspect the chain if symptoms call for it.
  - q: Do you work on the xDrive all-wheel-drive system?
    a: >-
      Yes. xDrive uses an electronically controlled multi-plate clutch for variable torque distribution; it needs transfer-case oil renewal at the specified intervals to avoid wind-up and premature wear. We diagnose system faults with compatible equipment.

## BMW technical areas we cover

- **Modular B-series engines** — B38 three-cylinder 1.5, B47 2.0 diesel, B48 2.0 petrol, B57 / B58 six-cylinder
- **TwinPower Turbo** — BMW's umbrella name for all turbocharged engines from 2011 onwards (single-turbo, twin-scroll or bi-turbo)
- **LongLife engine oil** — specifications LL-01, LL-04 and LL-12 FE depending on engine: we use the oil listed in the technical sheet
- **xDrive AWD** — all-wheel drive with electronically controlled multi-plate clutch
- **iDrive and CBS** — sensor-driven maintenance management
- **Adaptive diagnostics** — ECU coding and adaptation procedures after component replacement
