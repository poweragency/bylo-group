## What we do on Toyotas

- **Toyota Hybrid Service** with full HV system check (NiMH battery, inverter, electric motor)
- **Standard Toyota services** for petrol and diesel models (Hilux, Land Cruiser)
- **Techstream-compatible diagnostics** on Toyota systems (engine, hybrid, ABS, ESP, T-Mate ADAS)
- **Extended intervals** per Toyota's maintenance plan (15,000-30,000 km on petrol, specific intervals on HV)
- **Timing belt replacement** on models that require it (Hybrids use a chain, no belt)
- **Tyres and alignment** sized for the full SUV range (RAV4, Highlander, Land Cruiser)
- **T-Mate ADAS calibration** after windscreen or bumper replacement

## Toyota technical specifics

- **Hybrid Synergy Drive (HSD)** — full hybrid with two electric motors (MG1 / MG2) and a planetary power-split device
- **NiMH HV battery** — average life beyond 200,000 km, with per-cell diagnostics
- **Electric coolant pump** — replaces the conventional mechanical pump on HV engines
- **T-Mate** — Toyota's ADAS package (Pre-Collision, lane keeping, adaptive cruise)
