## Route summary

- **Origin**: Ospiate di Bollate (Milan) hub
- **Destination**: Bologna city, province, Emilia cluster up to Modena/Reggio Emilia
- **Distance**: ~210 km via A1 Milan-Bologna
- **Standard driving time**: 2-3 hours
- **ADR classes served**: 2, 3, 4.1, 4.2, 5.1, 5.2, 6.1, 8, 9

## Why this corridor works well

Milan-Bologna is one of the most optimized in our network:

- **Same-day round trip** — morning departure, morning delivery, afternoon return
- **Frequent groupage** to multiple Emilia consignees
- **Easy backhaul** — Emilia is rich in producers shipping north
- **Po Valley plain** — few geographical constraints, no critical ADR tunnels

## Sectors we move along Milan-Bologna

Emilia hosts major hubs in our served sectors:

- **Pharmaceutical** — Modena/Bologna pharma cluster (multinationals and CDMOs)
- **Fine chemistry and specialty chemicals** — Bologna-area specialist producers
- **Industrial machinery and packaging** — Emilian Packaging Valley cluster
- **Paints and coatings** for the Sassuolo tile district
- **Sports automotive** — suppliers to Ferrari, Lamborghini, Ducati, Pagani
