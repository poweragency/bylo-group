## Route summary

- **Origin**: Ospiate di Bollate hub (Milan)
- **Destination**: Turin and province
- **Distance**: ~140 km via A4 Milan-Turin
- **Standard driving time**: 1.5-2 hours
- **ADR classes served on this route**: 2, 3, 4.1, 4.2, 5.1, 5.2, 6.1, 8, 9

## Advantage of a short route

A 140 km route allows:

- **Same-day round trips** for the same tractor, reducing costs
- **Urgent deliveries** within hours of the request
- **Short stops** with lower exposure to overnight costs and ADR parking fees
- **High frequency**, useful for clients with continuous flows between the two industrial poles

For those moving ADR goods across the Lombardy-Piedmont triangle, this is BYLO Transport's most efficiency-per-cost route.
