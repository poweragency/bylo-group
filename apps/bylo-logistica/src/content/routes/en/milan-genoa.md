## Route summary

- **Origin**: Ospiate di Bollate (Milan) hub
- **Destination**: Port of Genoa, metropolitan area, Liguria riviera
- **Distance**: ~145 km via A7 Milan-Genoa
- **Standard driving time**: 2 hours (excluding ADR detours where required)
- **ADR classes served**: 2, 3, 4.1, 4.2, 5.1, 5.2, 6.1, 8, 9

## Tunnel planning

The A7 between Tortona and Genoa crosses a series of tunnels on the Liguria Apennine slopes. For ADR transport, planning is mandatory:

- **Tunnel category verification** for the specific ADR class of the product
- **Alternative routing** via Bocchetta or SS1 when needed
- **Port authority coordination** for pickups/deliveries inside terminal areas
- **Pre-prepared documentation** for the more frequent checks on this corridor

## Typical flows

The Port of Genoa is the destination for:

- **Import** containers with chemical raw materials, additives, industrial materials
- **Export** containers with finished goods from the Lombard industrial cluster
- Direct shipments to/from the **Liguria petrochemical cluster** (Iplom refinery in Busalla, Sannazzaro refinery)
- Movement of **lubricants and oils** for the Tigullio shipbuilding industry
