## What we bring to pharma

Pharma has unique constraints:

1. **Rigorous traceability** — every step in the chain must be documented.
2. **Tight timing** — production downtime is expensive, a missed reagent delivery blocks an analysis.
3. **Chemical safety** — many pharma substances fall under ADR classes (solvents, acids, reactive intermediates).

What we carry:

- Active pharmaceutical ingredients (APIs)
- Reagents for diagnostics and research
- Pharma-grade solvents (ethanol, isopropanol, acetone)
- Lab materials (glassware, single-use plastics)
- Synthesis intermediates
- Excipients and non-cold-chain raw materials

**What we don't handle**: finished medicines requiring cold chain (2-8°C or -20°C), ultra-low-temperature biologics, vaccines.
