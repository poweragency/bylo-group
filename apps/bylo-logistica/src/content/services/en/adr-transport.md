## What the service includes

ADR transport isn't a regular run: every trip has safety, regulatory and documentation implications that need precision.

- **Upfront load verification** — safety data sheet (SDS) review, ADR classification, vehicle compatibility check, compatibility with other goods on board.
- **Route planning** — some routes have ADR restrictions (tunnels, urban centers, time slots). We plan accordingly.
- **On-board documentation** — DDT, ADR regulation, safety data sheet, written driver instructions for emergencies.
- **Delivery certification** — at delivery, the consignee signs the handover under ADR rules.

## Who it's for

Companies producing or using substances in the served ADR classes: chemicals and petrochemicals, pharmaceutical and diagnostics, automotive (oils, batteries, paints), construction (paints and solvents).

If you're unsure whether your cargo requires ADR transport, contact us: a verification takes 5 minutes starting from the product technical sheet.
