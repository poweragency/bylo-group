## What you can store

- Palletized industrial goods (in boxes, drums, bags, big bags)
- Technical components and spare parts (single SKUs or service kits)
- Non-perishable chemicals in the served ADR classes
- Production raw materials (awaiting consumption or re-launch)
- Safety stock for branches or direct customers

## Strategic location advantage

Ospiate di Bollate is a natural logistics hub for Northern Italy: three motorway exits within a few km (A4 Milan-Turin, A8 Milan-Lakes, western Milan ring road), access to the Milano-Smistamento freight yard, and proximity to Malpensa and Linate airports for intermodal shipments.

For distributors covering Lombardy, Piedmont, Liguria and Veneto, our site cuts average delivery times compared to facilities south of Milan.
